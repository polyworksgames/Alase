Our Mission

The Mission of Alase Center for Enrichment is to improve the quality of life and strengthen individuals, families and communities through the provision of direct mental health services. The center will provide a model of economic self-sufficiency within our community, specifically regarding mental health services. We use a multi modal approach to our treatment that considers the uniqueness of each entity. Ultimately we strive to provide a safe, comfortable and nurturing place where growth and transformation can occur.

Our Services

Our work follows the Sequential Intercept Model, providing support at key touchpoints where care and community can make the greatest impact. Whether it's accessing housing, enrolling in school, finding a job, or simply feeling heard, Alase walks alongside each individual with compassion and consistency. At Alase, we do more than support transitions — we build bridges to stability, relationships that heal, and legacies that empower. Every step forward becomes part of a larger story, one written not by systems, but by the people we serve.

One-on-One Mentoring

Personalized guidance from experienced mentors who understand your unique journey and challenges.

  • Weekly scheduled sessions
  • Goal-setting and tracking
  • Confidential support

Re-Entry Services

Comprehensive support for individuals returning to the community after incarceration — housing, employment, and life skills.

  • Housing placement assistance
  • Employment readiness
  • Community integration

Virtual Mentoring

Flexible online mentoring options that accommodate busy schedules and geographical constraints.

  • Video conferencing
  • Digital resources
  • 24/7 messaging support

Professional Development

Career-focused mentoring to help you advance professionally and achieve your career goals.

  • Resume and interview prep
  • Industry networking
  • Leadership training
