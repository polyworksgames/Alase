Executive Leadership

Anthony Smith, PhD

Executive Director / Psychologist

Dr. Anthony Smith is the Executive Director and licensed Psychologist at Alase Center for Enrichment. With extensive expertise in clinical psychology and community-based mental health services, Dr. Smith leads the organization's mission to heal minds, hearts, and souls across North Carolina.

Dr. Smith's approach is grounded in trauma-informed care, evidence-based treatment, and a deep commitment to serving marginalized populations — including individuals navigating the criminal justice system, re-entry, and specialized treatment needs. His leadership bridges clinical excellence with compassionate community service, ensuring that every person who walks through Alase's doors receives dignified, effective, and holistic care.

Under Dr. Smith's guidance, Alase has developed specialized programs in re-entry support, peer mentoring, and sex offender treatment that adhere to the highest clinical and ethical standards. He believes strongly that transformation is possible for everyone, and that with the right support, individuals can rebuild their lives and contribute meaningfully to their communities.
